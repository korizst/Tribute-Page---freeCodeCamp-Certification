Michael Schumacher

The first ever seven-time Formula-1 World Champion in the history

Here's a time line of Michael Schumacher's life:

  • 1969 - Born in Hürth-Hermülheim, Germany
  • 1973 - He gets his first go-kart from his parents
  • 1975 - Becomes the champion of the kart club in Kerpen
  • 1984 - Wins the German junior championship in karting
  • 1985 - Second place in the junior go-kart world championship
  • 1986 - he finished third in the German adult go-kart championship and the adult European championship
  • 1987 - German champion and European champion
  • 1988 - Starts racing in Formula 1 training series
  • 1991 - First ever race in Formula-1
  • 1992 - First ever win in Formula-1 in Spa-Francorchamps
  • 1994 - 1995 - Becomes World Champion
  • 1996 - Joins Scuderia Ferrari
  • 2000 - 2004 - Wins the World Champions title in five consecutive years
  • 2006 - After losing the championship battle against Fernando Alonso he announces his first retirement
  • 2010 - He comes back as the driver of Mercedes
  • 2012 - Final retirement from Formula-1
  • 2013 - He sufferes a serious skiing accident in France, and his family has keep his true state of health a secret ever since
